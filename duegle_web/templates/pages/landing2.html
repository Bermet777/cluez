About Us

Cluez.io is an AI-enabled bad news engine for small and medium businesses looking to mitigate their risks.

  • Trusted by reputed financial and non-financial companies
  • Available in six languages and six regions
  • Fast as well as cost effective

Our mission is to help small teams make informed business decisions about their customers without spending a fortune on due diligence. We plan to achieve this by capitalizing on machine power to increase the speed and accuracy of information processing.

  • What is due dilligence?

    Due diligence is the reasonable steps taken by your business, to verify the information provided by your suppliers, debtors and creditors, while doing a business transaction, sometimes as a part of law compliance, to avoid committing an offence.

  • Which languages and regions do you currently serve?

    We currently serve in six different languages and regions English, German, Hungarian, French, Polish and Spanish.
